Where the name comes from

Jatin Chopra

I like knowing where my own name comes from, so here's my short version of it.

Jatin
first name

One of the names for Shiva — the god of destruction, but also, in the stories I grew up with, the god who is easiest to please. I've always liked that combination: something that can tear down and rebuild, and doesn't need much to be content.

Chopra
family name

A surname tied to the Khatri community of Punjab — historically associated with trade and soldiering, people who showed up for a fight when it mattered. I like to think some of that shows up in how I approach a stubborn dataset.

Academic path
