Research work

How Galaxies Work?

My research is primarily focused on understanding galaxy evolution and the physics of the interstellar medium. In my master's thesis, I'm investigating whether post-merger galaxies stop forming stars any differently from non-interacting galaxies.

MSc Thesis · Ongoing

Star Formation quenching in post-mergers

Comparing 388 post-merger galaxies to 7,051 matched controls using MaNGA IFU spectroscopy, building radial ΔΣSFR profiles from both Hα and D4000-based star-formation indicators.
