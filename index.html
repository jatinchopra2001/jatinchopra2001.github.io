Current work

Resolved quenching in post-merger galaxies

My MSc thesis at AIfA asks a spatial question: after two galaxies merge, does star formation shut off everywhere at once, or does it shut off from the inside out? I build matched control samples and radial star-formation profiles across ~10,070 MaNGA galaxies to find out.

MSc Thesis · Ongoing

Spatially resolved quenching in post-mergers

Comparing 388 post-merger galaxies to 7,051 matched controls using MaNGA IFU spectroscopy, building radial ΔΣSFR profiles from both Hα and D4000-based star-formation indicators.

Cloud conditions from molecular abundances

Building a neural-network surrogate in JAX and running NumPyro-based Bayesian inference to recover initial cloud conditions — temperature, density, cosmic-ray ionisation, UV field — from observed molecular abundances.
